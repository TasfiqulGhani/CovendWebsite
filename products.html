Product Information

The CoVend test kit contains six components:

Cassette Test Card, Pipette, Lancet, Diluent, Alcohol Wipe & Instruction Leaflet. Contents of the kit bag are enclosed in a sealed bag to prevent cross contamination.


HIGH QUALITY & DESIGN

The Cassette Test Card has dedicated nitrocellulose membrane strips for detecting the IgM / IgG antibodies separately and returns more accurate results compared to a combined strip in some inferior products. The high quality material used in the CoVend Rapid Test Kit for the nitrocellulose membrane strips is also a critical factor for the accuracy of RAPID results within 15 minutes.

Our Testing Kits Conform to CE Standards and are fully FDA approved :

EASE OF USE:

The CoVend Test Kit is designed to be user friendly and straight forward so that it can be used at home, oï¬ƒce or in a hospital environment, without professional attendance. Everything needed for the test is contained in the kit.

Clean your finger with the alcohol wipe and use the lancet to create pin hole size puncture on your finger. Collected the blood sample by using the Pipette and place 1 drop of blood (10ul) into each of the receptor(S) of the Cassette Test Card, followed by two drops(100ul) of saline diluent. The user waits 15 minutes for results.
