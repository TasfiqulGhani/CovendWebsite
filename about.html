The CoVend Story

CoVend is an Irish Company formed in 2020 with the aim of making Covid-19 rapid testing more readily available to the public. Through a partnership with the developers of the most accurate testing kit available in the market we have been licensed to distribute their product under the CoVend Brand.


From the beginning of the Covid-19 pandemic our team at CoVend have been working to achieve a package which will make an impact in the global fight against this virus. We believe we have delivered this through the CoVend Rapid Test Kit and App.

Being able to test and receive a result within minutes will offer an individual the freedom of “knowing” through our app we hope to offer the world the freedom of “going”.

Having the ability to collect and correlate data from individuals who have been tested is crucial to understanding the spread of COVID-19 and the steps which can be taken to allow us to return to some form of normalcy.

We believe that the CoVend App can be the bridge which helps with the return to schooling, work and a vibrant economy. Through offering anyone using our test the chance to play their part in the fight against Covid-19 by uploading their test results anonymously.

When a user logs their result to our App we instantly update our world information. This will help society understand more quickly the risk, or indeed lack of risk, of infection in any given area. A look at our online statistics will show the latest results and statistical information. We also offer live feeds from the World Health Organisation and the Centre for Disease Control. Our tutorial explains how simple it is to take our test.
